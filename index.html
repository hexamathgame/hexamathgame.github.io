Game FAQs

  • How do I solve a HexaMath level? Tap three tiles to create an equation. If the sum of those tiles matches the target number, you're closer to completing the level. Find all possible combinations to achieve the target number.
  • Can I reset my progress? No
  • Are there in-app purchases? Currently, HexaMath is free with no in-app purchases, but they may be added in future updates.
  • How do I unlock new levels? Complete the previous level to unlock the next one automatically.

Troubleshooting

  • Game Crashes: Ensure your iOS is updated and reinstall HexaMath from the App Store if issues persist.
  • Puzzle Won't Load: Check your internet connection or restart the app.
  • Progress Not Saving: Enable iCloud sync in the game settings to back up your progress.
